A little about my work

I integrate plant phenomics, quantitative genetics, plant breeding, and ML/AI-driven approaches to improve crop performance and resilience.

My work focuses on building scalable, field-based breeding strategies that combine genomic, phenomic, and environmental data to accelerate genetic gain and support sustainable, climate-resilient crop improvement. I currently work as a Data Scientist - Data Integrity and Temperate Hub Phenomics Lead at Syngenta. I completed my Ph.D. through the Winter Wheat Breeding Program at South Dakota State University. This site is a living record of my journey, projects, reading, and the ideas I'm excited about.
