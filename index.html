01 — About

A little about my work

I integrate quantitative genetics, plant breeding, and data-driven approaches to improve crop performance and resilience. My research combines UAV-based high-throughput phenotyping, genomics, and machine/deep learning to model complex traits and genotype × environment interactions for more accurate selection decisions.

I focus on building scalable, field-based breeding strategies that fuse genomic, phenomic, and environmental data to accelerate genetic gain and enable sustainable, climate-resilient crop improvement. Right now I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Winter Wheat Breeding Program at South Dakota State University. This site is a living record of my journey, projects, reading, and the things I'm proud of.
